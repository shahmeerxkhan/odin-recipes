Mac and Cheese Recipe





Ingredients:

  • Macaroni: 2 cups of elbow macaroni or your preferred pasta shape.
  • Butter: ¼ cup (half a stick) for the roux and topping.
  • Flour: ¼ cup all-purpose flour to help thicken the sauce.
  • Milk: 2 cups whole milk for a rich, creamy base.
  • Cheese: 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese, plus extra for topping.
  • Salt and Pepper: To taste, for seasoning the sauce.
  • Breadcrumbs (optional): ½ cup for a crunchy baked topping.

Steps:

  1. Boil the pasta: Cook macaroni in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Make the roux: In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Stir in flour and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Add milk: Gradually whisk in the milk, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens.
  4. Add cheese: Remove from heat and stir in shredded cheddar until fully melted and smooth. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Combine: Add cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce and mix until well coated.
  6. Bake (optional): Transfer to a baking dish, top with extra cheese and breadcrumbs, and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 20 minutes or until golden and bubbly.
